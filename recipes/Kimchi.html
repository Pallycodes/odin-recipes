Kimchi

Description

Kimchi is a korean dish composed of fermented napa cabage and spices for the ultimate umami flavor.

Ingredients:

  • kosher salt
  • 1 napa cabbage
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 daikon radish
  • 1 bunch of green onions

Paste ingredients:

  • 1 asian pear
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 2 inch piece of ginger
  • 1/4 cup of fish sauce
  • 1/3 cup of korean red pepper flakes

Steps:

  1. Wash cabage and dry off.
  2. Chop cabage and green onion.
  3. Juliene raddish and carrots.
  4. Heavly salt cabage and let sit for 2 hours.
  5. while we wait for cabage put all paste ingredients into blender and blend.
  6. After 2 hours wash all salt of cabage.
  7. Mix paste,cabage,carrots, and radish together.
  8. Store in container or glass jar and wait for 4-6 days for cabage to ferment at room temperature.
  9. After 4-6 days Enjoy!!
