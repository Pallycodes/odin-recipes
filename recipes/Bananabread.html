Banana Bread

Description

Delicious and moist banana bread made from ripped bananas.A perfect energy packed snack.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups mashed ripped bananas
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups of flour

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350F and butter loaf pan.
  2. Mash bananas and add butter.
  3. Mix rest of ingredients.
  4. Bake bread for 55 to 65 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  5. Let bread cool and Enjoy!!
